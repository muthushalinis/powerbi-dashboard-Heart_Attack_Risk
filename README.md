This project presents an interactive dashboard analysing heart attack risk factors using a Russia-based health dataset.
The dashboard focuses on understanding how demographic, lifestyle, and health conditions relate to heart attack risk.

It covers:

- Overall heart attack risk distribution across the population  
- Risk breakdown by age group and gender  
- Impact of lifestyle factors such as smoking, alcohol consumption, physical activity, and stress  
- Relationship between obesity levels and heart attack risk  
- Risk distribution across income levels and employment status  
- Summary of key medical conditions (hypertension, diabetes, prior heart attack, obesity)

Key metrics include:

- Average heart rate  
- Average blood pressure  
- Average cholesterol levels
Interactive filters allow users to explore risk patterns by gender and other categories.

Tools used:

- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Data modelling and visualisation
